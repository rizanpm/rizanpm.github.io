CURTAIN WALL SYSTEMS

SLIM SERIES SY-35

SY35 is a fairly new addition to the stick curtain wall product line of SYKON. It addresses the need for a slim line captured glazed curtain wall system that provides a better sightline for the facade of the building. The width of the SY35 as the name suggests is only 35 millimeters. This is around 40% less of the standard curtain wall system width in the market. Of course, aside from the cost savings from the reduced frame width, there is the aesthetic advantage of having a slender frame offering wider sightline and sleek appearance that a lot of architects and designers prefer. Not to mention the better thermal conductivity of the system due to its minimal width. The system caters to standard glass thicknesses and up to 50 millimeters of glazing should the project require it.
It offers a wide range of typology for what suits the project design. Either, simple strip curtain wall or a continuous curtain wall with single glazed spandrels, the system performs high serviceability standards. The accessories and hardware used on every SYKON product are meticulously screened to provide the best performance under which the system is tested. SY35 are commonly used for resort hotels and vacation villas.

CURTAIN WALL SYSTEMS

SYK-80-UNITIZED

SYK-80 – Unitized curtain wall an innovative facade system, which is especially preferred on high-rise buildings. This system is completely fabricated in plant with direct bonding silicone feature and installed in a shorter time without scaffold, unitized facade system enables fast and easy installation without additional cassette cost and labour. Unitized facade system is customized & designed on the project basis. With high performance features, all the fabrication, including the glass, made in plant with high quality provides tolerance gap on seismic moves and also high-performance features. For internal joint gab appearance, it offers various options with Aluminium profile or black that has curtain wall colour and contrast depending on architectural choice. Structural Glazing, Two Side, 4 sided & with Cap alternatives in standard curtain wall systems are also available in unitized facade systems. Designed to suit modern construction methods for medium and high rise residential or commercial buildings, combines enhanced energy efficiency with design versatility and proven weather resistant performance. With trusted German engineering and performance testing, excellent water tightness, strength, and airtightness.
