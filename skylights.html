Skylights

Skylights are architectural features designed to introduce natural light into a building by incorporating transparent or translucent windows into the roof. These windows are strategically placed to allow sunlight to filter into interior spaces, creating a brighter and more open atmosphere. Skylights are popular in residential homes as they offer numerous benefits. They not only enhance the aesthetics of a space by providing a visual connection to the sky, but they also contribute to energy efficiency by reducing the need for artificial lighting during daylight hours. Additionally, skylights can improve ventilation and air circulation in a home. While skylights bring the beauty of natural light indoors, it's important to consider proper installation and design to control factors such as heat gain, glare, and potential leaks. Well-placed and thoughtfully designed skylights can significantly enhance the overall living experience within a house.
