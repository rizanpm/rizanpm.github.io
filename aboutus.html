About our Industry

ALTAL INTERNATIONAL was established in the year 2010 at Al Batinah South, Oman. In over 13 years of experience in the fabrication of high-quality aluminium profiles systems for windows, doors, facades, glazed roofs, sun-rooms. Since 2010 we have successfully executed high quality architectural aluminium and glass works for residential and commercial building all over Sultanate of Oman.

High standards are ensured by the efforts of our professional and experienced managers and engineers.

Our workshop and processing facilities includes highly qualified and experienced team of technical,personel and skilled work force utilizing a fleet of latest production machines, equipments and resources.

Our management team ensures a successful transition from contract award to design, procurement, fabrication,installation to final completion are provided to our valued customers.

ALTAL INTERNATIONAL stands out for its consistent delivery of innovative and customized solutions, blending creativity with technical expertise to create unique and precisely tailored designs for each project.

The company has to its credit many prestigious projects, all over Oman . A comprehensive list of completed jobs is presented here to reflect the varies types of projects that we have participated in and the consultants we have worked with. Our confidence in being able to execute diverse projects can be attributed to our skilled technicians, coupled with our long tanding experience in the regional market.
